## 1. Introduction

BiteWise ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the BiteWise mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### Data You Provide Directly
- Name and email address (account registration)
- Height, weight, age, gender, and activity level (onboarding)
- Body composition goal and dietary preferences
- Food photos and meal descriptions (for AI nutritional analysis)
- Manual food entries and nutritional data
- Body weight log entries

### Data Collected Automatically
- App usage interactions and feature engagement
- Device information and operating system version
- Crash reports and performance diagnostics

### Payment Data
Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers. Stripe may collect billing name and payment method information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- To provide, operate, and personalize the App's nutrition tracking features
- To analyze food photos using AI to estimate caloric and macro content
- To calculate personalized calorie and macronutrient goals
- To track your progress, streaks, and health trends over time
- To send meal reminders and push notifications (only if you grant permission)
- To process and manage subscription payments
- To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
- To comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. AI-Based Food Analysis

When you upload food photos or enter descriptions, this data is transmitted to third-party AI service providers for real-time nutritional analysis. This processing is ephemeral — image data is used only to fulfill your specific request and is not retained by AI providers beyond that request. We do not use your food images to train AI models without your explicit consent.

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

**Service Providers:** We share data with third-party vendors that help us operate the App, including AI providers (for food analysis), Stripe (for payment processing), and analytics/crash reporting tools. These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only as necessary to provide their services.

**Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BiteWise, our users, or the public.

**Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

---

## 6. Data Safety (Google Play)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, here is a summary of our data practices:

| Data Category | Collected | Shared | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Personal Info (name, email) | Yes | No | Account management |
| Health & Fitness (weight, etc.) | Yes | No | App functionality, analytics |
| Photos (food images) | Yes | Yes* | AI food analysis (ephemeral) |
| App activity & interactions | Yes | No | Analytics, app improvement |
| Payment info | No | No | Handled directly by Stripe |

\* Shared with third-party AI providers for ephemeral processing only.

All collected data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS). Users can request data deletion at any time from the Profile page.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account via the Profile page, we will delete your associated data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (also available in-app).
- **Portability:** Request a portable copy of your data in machine-readable form.
- **Opt-out:** Opt out of non-essential notifications or communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: [support@bitewise.app](mailto:support@bitewise.app)

---

## 9. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS) and secure infrastructure. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any confirmed breach as required by law.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

BiteWise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has used our App, please contact [support@bitewise.app](mailto:support@bitewise.app).

---

## 11. Push Notifications

BiteWise may send push notifications for meal reminders and streak updates. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the App's Profile page. Notification functionality is available only on eligible subscription plans.

---

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
